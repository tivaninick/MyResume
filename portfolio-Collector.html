Collector Monitor Dashboad

This is the Collector Monitor Dashboad Project

This custom-built dashboard delivers actionable insights into the behavior and performance of collectors deployed on the Aveksa platform. It provides a centralized view of collector status, covering Identity Collectors, Account Collectors, and other collector types. With this dashboard, administrators can:

- Monitor collector performance: Track collector status, collection rates, and error rates in real-time.
- Identify potential issues: Quickly detect collector failures, data inconsistencies, or other issues that may impact identity governance.
- Optimize collection processes: Use data-driven insights to refine collector configurations, improve collection efficiency, and reduce errors.

By providing a unified view of collector behavior, this dashboard enables the organization to optimize their identity governance processes, ensure data accuracy, and reduce risk.
